# 2D Animation
Create themed art asset packs which include storyboards, animations, and environments

You may collaborate with other students for ideas asset packs as long as it is documented on who completed which assets

# Game Development
Create a 2D Platformer using art assets created by 2D Animation students

You may work together in small groups of up to 3 (4 for us) development students but expectations will be much higher with a larger team

Mr. Wong has the final decision on who works in a team or not
